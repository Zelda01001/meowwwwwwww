USSR

The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (USSR) was a state that existed from December 30, 1922 to December 26, 1991. It occupied the eastern half of Europe and the northern third of Asia. The capital was Moscow.

Vodka was a significant part of Soviet culture and was often consumed during toasts to celebrate various occasions

The Soviet flag featured a hammer and sickle, symbolizing the unity of workers and peasants

The Trans-Siberian Railway, spanning over 9,000 kilometers, is the longest railway line in the world
